Ongoing Research

Investigating the Factors Associated with Death Registration in Zambia

This study examines the socio-demographic, geographic, and household-level factors influencing death registration in Zambia using a mixed-methods approach. Quantitative analysis is based on the 2022 Zambia Population and Housing Census, complemented by qualitative insights from community stakeholders.

The research aims to generate evidence to inform policy interventions for strengthening Zambia’s Civil Registration and Vital Statistics (CRVS) system, with particular focus on rural–urban disparities and vulnerable populations.

Methods

  • Descriptive and inferential analysis of census microdata
  • Logistic regression modelling
  • Qualitative thematic analysis
  • Policy-oriented interpretation

Key Themes

  • CRVS system performance
  • Mortality data completeness
  • Geographic and socio-economic disparities
  • Health system integration
